Ball Toss

Role:

Lead Game Designer and Developer

Date:

October 2023

Context:

University of Colorado Boulder

















Description:

  "Ball Toss" is a Unity and Google Cardboard VR mini-game I developed to showcase my growth in VR game development, with a focus on physics for heavy objects, object materials and textures, and dynamic movement and interaction. The primary goal of this project was to bring together all the technical skills we had been learning about to create a visually engaging 3D experience.

Please note that the 3D assets are not mine. This mini-game is a personal interpretation for skill practice.
