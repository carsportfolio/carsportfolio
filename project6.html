Sleepy Stuffing

Role:

Lead Animator and Artist

Date:

May 2023

Context:

University of Colorado Boulder

















Description:

  "Sleepy Stuffing" is an animated video I created for an assignment, portraying a daydream sequence where I transition from reality into a surreal, imagined life. The primary goal of this project was to get some practice in Adobe After Effect + Adobe Premiere Pro. I also used this opportunity to refine my digital art skills by illustrating the backgrounds using Adobe Photoshop.

The Artist Statement and more was originally uploaded to a website which features this assignment and more.

Please note that Stitches, Animal Crossing: New Horizons, and the Nintendo Switch are trademarks of Nintendo. This video is not affiliated with or endorsed by Nintendo and is a personal artistic interpretation for skill practice.
