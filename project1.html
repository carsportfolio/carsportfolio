Sculpt Them All: Wooper

Role:

Lead Object Designer, 3D Modeler, and Renderer

Date:

April 2022

Context:

University of Colorado Boulder

















Description:

  "Sculpt Them All: Wooper" is my take on a simple figurine of one of my favorite characters from the Pokémon franchise. The product was sculpted using AutoDesk Fusion and 3D rendered in Adobe Dimension. The main thing I wanted to focus on with this project was combining static and fluid shapes to create something cohesive. This project demonstrates software capabilities as well as creative modeling and rendering.
