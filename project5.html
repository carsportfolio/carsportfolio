Finfillment

Role:

Lead UX, UI, and Game Designer & Developer

Date:

August 2023 - May 2024

Context:

University of Colorado Boulder - Capstone

















Description:

  "Finfillment" is an interactive game concept that aims to help persuade adolescents into forming long-term habits from goals through game progression. The game was designed as a concept in Adobe XD and was planned to have a Unity (C#) release. The primary goal of this concept and game design was not completion, but to showcase proficiency in a wide range of skills, demonstrating my qualifications for earning my degree.

Progress was originally uploaded to a website which features sketches, videos, Unity interactions, and more!
